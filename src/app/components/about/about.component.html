About the Quran

The Quran is the holy book of Islam and is regarded by Muslims as the unaltered word of God (Allah), revealed through the Prophet Muhammad. It consists of 114 chapters, known as Surahs, which are further divided into verses (Ayat).

The Quran was transmitted orally during Muhammad's lifetime and compiled shortly after his death. Muslims view its preservation and authenticity as a testament to divine care.
